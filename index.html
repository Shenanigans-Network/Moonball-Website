    OUR HISTORY

    Moonball Network has been around for over 1 year now. What started off as a small SMP Server with friends has developed into something we couldn't even imagine!

    OUR SERVERS

    We have Bedwars, Survival, Skyblock and Duels on our network, all with fully custom builds and useful perks and features. Our servers ensure 20TPS at all times!

    OUR GAMES

    SURVIVAL

    Survival is a popular Vanilla gamemode where you claim land, build a base with your friends, and get better armor and tools. We have several awesome features such as /home and a handy shop GUI. Compete with other players strive to become the richest player!

    SKYBLOCK

    Skyblock is a gamemode where you start on a floating island with minimal resources. Compete with others or join them and jump to the top of the leaderboards! Difficult challenges are set for you through your sky adventure, complete them for rewards! It takes perseverance, dedication, and effort to get to the top!

    DUELS

    Duels is a competitive, fast-paced gamemode with 1v1s with players! Chose your kit and join the race to the top of the leaderboard. Explore our 6 custom maps and test your 1.8 PvP skills. Join the fight today on Moonball Network!

    BEDWARS

    Bedwars is a very competitive, popular gamemode consisting of 4 or 8 teams a game, trying to break each other's beds and kill the opponents within the time limit! Play Solos and Duos on our 4 maps and test your 1.8 PvP skills today on the Moonball Network
